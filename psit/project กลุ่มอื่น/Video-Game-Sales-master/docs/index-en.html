Overview

Categorizing by platform type, the most video games platform type available is home video game consoles. This chart is kinda clear, because in 80s and 90s, PCs are mainly designed for working, not gaming. That's why there're so many console games.

Categorizing by genre, you'll see that the most video games genre available is action. The second is the sports genre. One of the reasons that makes action games the most interested is that action games are usually challenging, fun and exciting for everybody.

Releases

Now, let's look deeper. You'll be able to see how many video games are released in each year from 1980 to 2016.

Previous
Next

According to the chart, the most popular genre for publishing is action, just because action games are considered to be least complicated and easy to play when compared to other genres. Unlike simulation, which players need some time to learn how to play and master it.

The second most popular genre for publishing is sports, the year that sports games are published more than usual usually have big event about sports, such as 2002 has 2002 FIFA World Cup and 2008 has Beijing 2008 Summer Olympics, which make people more interested in sports, so video game publishers may chose to publish their sport games during these years.

Another popular genre we'll be talking about is shooter. The releases of shooter games are quite high in 2008, which actually started to rise since 2002. There're some best-selling and high-rating shooter games in early 2000s, for example: Halo: Combat Evolved (2001), Medal of Honor: Frontline (2002) and Halo 2 (2004). This could make other video game publishers tend to make more shooter games in the future.

Another factor that affects the releases are new releases of gaming platforms, for example: PlayStation 2 (2000), Nintendo DS (2004), Wii (2005), XBox360 (2005) and PlayStation 3 (2006). During these years, video games are published more than normal, which were opportunities to video game publishers to release their games on several platforms or just release the new one to boost their sales.

As you can see, there're not just only the increasing part, so let's talk about the decreasing part. You'll see that during 2010-2013, there has been a huge drop of releases due to the increment of smartphones influence. This is one of the factors that affects releases of video games, so video game publishers may tend to release them on smartphone platforms more than before, because they're portable and many people owns it. For example: In 2009, 'Plants vs. Zombies' has been released on many platforms, but later in 2013, 'Plants vs. Zombies 2' has only been released on iOS and Android.

Summary: Many external factors affects video game releases. For example: players' passion, events, previous games' ratings, releases of new consoles or even technological influence. These are the reasons why developers should decide what should they create.

Growth

Next, let's see how much gaming industry has grown for decades.

Starting in the 80s, most of the video games are on home video game consoles. When time passes, handheld game consoles and PC games are starting to get more attention. One of the reasons might be the lag of technology. During 80s and 90s, it wasn't easy to make small gaming device and PCs weren't designed for gaming, but since the year 2000, technology are now more advanced, so the amount of video games on PCs and handheld game consoles are starting to rise significantly till today.

Summary: Technology is crucial. It's the reason that keeps the world moving forward. Just look at the chart above, gaming industry has grown this far due to the modern technology.

According to the chart, you'll obviously notice that there're so many curves, up and down. This somehow related to the releases chart, but still, let's take a deeper look on some events.

  • In 1984 there's the game named Duck Hunt which has 28.31 million sales (count as 56.22% of that year sales)
  • In 1985 there's the game named Super Mario Bros. which has 40.24 million sales (count as 74.6% of that year sales)
  • In 1989 there's the game named Tetris which has 30.26 million sales and the game named Super Mario Land which has 18.14 million sales(both games count as 65.89% of that year sales)
  • In 1995, the first event of Electronic Entertainment Expo was held in Los Angeles Convention Center. In this event, there will be many game publishers gathering up and set up their own exhibition in the event, which could make people more interested in new video games and boost their sales up.
  • On October 2000, Sony released PlayStation 2. This boosts video game sales up in 2001-2002.
  • On November 2004, Nintendo DS was released. On November 2005, Wii and XBox360 were released. On November 2006, PlayStation 3 was released. The releases of these 4 brand new and popular gaming platforms significantly boost the video game sales up from 2004 to 2008.

Summary: Video game sales will be greatly high for about 1-3 years before dropping down due to new console releases. This somehow decreases the sales of older platforms. Researches also show that video game sales usually rises after 1-2 years after the releases of new consoles, because developers need to study the platform before making new games. In addition, if we can make games that match the people's favor just right, that game might get unbelievable sale.

Summary

From everything above, what do we get?

What do we know more?

  • From the Releases section, we get to know that there're so many external factors that affect game releases in each year. Most effective factors are the releases of new consoles and technological influence.
  • From the Growth section, we get to know the importance of technology, which made gaming industry grown this far.
  • From the Sales section, we get to know that video game sales of each year are most affected from releases amount. Also, if we can make games that match the people's favor just right, that game might get unbelievable sale.
  • From the Publishers section, we get to know the reason why top 16 video game publishers are able to dominate the market roughly 81.75%, while the other 562 publishers only own a total of 18.25% of all sales. The reason is their very own strength or highlight of their games.
  • We've found out that we might be able to catagorize the gaming eras.
    • 1980-1994 - In this period, gaming are not much popular. Technology isn't also that advanced, so not so many games and platforms.
    • 1995-2009 - In this period, gaming went boom. Many new consoles are released, following by tons of video games.
    • 2009-Now - In this period, smartphones and modern technology now have more influence to our lives. Developers now tend to release their game on mobile platforms instead. New gaming platform types are getting born, for example: hybrid type (like Nintendo Switch) or virtual reality (VR).


How do we apply them to our lifes?

  • If you're going to be a game developer, game designer or whatever. We now know many factors that affects the sale, so you should be planning carefully on making the game with these questions.
    • Which genre should we do?
    • Which platform should we do it?
    • When should it be released?
    • What is the sale point or highlight of the game?
  • We get to know that it's important to learn about new technologies. The more you know, more chance you'll be able to succeed in this industry, like following news about techonologies or stay tuned to new gaming platform announcements.
  • From the Publishers section, we get to know about key to success. It's important for every video game publishers to have the strength or highlights of their own, just like you and everyone else. If you're able to master at something, there will be a high chance that you'll succeed. In the chart, every video game publishers have both bad-selling games and good-selling games. This tells us that no one always succeed every attempts. That makes not giving up to be one of the keys to success.
